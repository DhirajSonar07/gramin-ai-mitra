## 1. Executive Summary
Gramin AI Mitra is a voice-first AI assistant designed to help rural citizens discover and understand government welfare schemes they are eligible for.

## 2. Problem Statement
Rural citizens face difficulty accessing government schemes due to language barriers, complex eligibility criteria, low digital literacy, and misinformation.

## 3. Target Users
- Rural citizens
- Small and marginal farmers
- Low-income households

## 4. Goals
- Simplify scheme discovery
- Provide personalized eligibility guidance
- Reduce misinformation
- Improve awareness of welfare programs

## 5. Functional Requirements
1. Voice and text-based interaction.
2. Guided eligibility questionnaire.
3. Scheme recommendation based on eligibility.
4. Document checklist generation.
5. Step-by-step application guidance.
6. Interaction logging for monitoring.

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements
- Scalable architecture
- Secure data handling
- Low-latency responses
- Cost-effective prototype

## 7. Success Metrics
- Reduced scheme discovery time
- Increased awareness
- Improved application accuracy

## 8. Responsible AI
- RAG-based grounded responses
- Guardrails to prevent hallucination
- Transparency disclaimer
- No permanent storage of sensitive data
